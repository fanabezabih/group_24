Input

Doctor names and specialties
Patient details (name, medical need, preferred time, specialty)
Appointment duration
Emergency cases (normal case,emergency case)        

Process
Store doctor schedules
Match patients to appropriate doctors based on availability, specialty, and preferences
Schedule appointments without conflicts
Insert emergency appointments by preempting or shifting lower-priority ones
Sort schedules by time and priority

output

List of scheduled appointments per doctor (ordered by time)
Priority queue of emergency cases
